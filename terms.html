Summarize This Terms of Use

Summarize This provides AI-assisted and local-rule Trello card summaries for review by the user. Output is a decision-support draft, not a guarantee that a Trello card is correct, complete, or ready to act on.

User responsibility

  • Review summaries, decisions, blockers, and exports before relying on them.
  • Confirm sensitive client, financial, legal, or personal information before sending it to an AI provider.
  • Keep provider API keys and Trello access under the control of authorized users only.

Availability

The Power-Up is delivered as static files. Provider availability, Trello access, hosting uptime, and browser restrictions can affect behavior.

Trello actions

Generated Trello comments or exports should be reviewed before use. The Power-Up is designed to require explicit approval before Trello write actions.
